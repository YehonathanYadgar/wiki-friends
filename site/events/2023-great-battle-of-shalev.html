2023 Great Battle Of Shalev

The final high-school-trip dune fight near Rishon LeZion where Jony became Obi-Wan, Shalev became Anakin, and the group gained one of its central tragedies.

Canon Setting

  • Time: 2023, during the final high school trip.
  • Place: dunes around Rishon LeZion.
  • Exact date: unknown.
  • Mythological frame: Anakin vs Obi-Wan on Mustafar.
  • Canon roles: Shalev = Anakin; Jony = Obi-Wan.
  • Reason: Jony had the high ground on the dune.

What Happened

Some group members, including Eitan, Tomer, and Alon, were play-fighting for fun.

Shalev wanted to battle Jony. Jony initially refused, but eventually they started a "fun fight" on the dunes.

During the fight:

  • Jony repeatedly pushed Shalev down the steep dune.
  • Shalev kept climbing back up.
  • Jony kept yelling You are not at Alole! as a joke reference.
  • Shalev got genuinely angry from repeatedly climbing and getting pushed down.

Then it escalated:

  • Shalev tried to punch Jony.
  • Jony blocked the punch and accidentally elbowed him.
  • Shalev's mouth started bleeding.

After they briefly stopped:

  • Shalev tried to choke Jony from behind.
  • Jony escaped, choked him back, and they rolled down the dune.

After another attempted truce:

  • Shalev attempted a strong kick toward Jony while they were sitting.
  • Alon slightly pushed him.
  • The push caused the kick to miss and probably prevented serious damage.

This became the legendary Great Battle of Shalev.

Why It Became Lore

Before the battle, Shalev was quiet, not central, but useful and liked: the kind of person who was often included when another player was needed for games. Jony personally liked him a lot because Shalev was chill, unambitious, drama-free, and usually available to play.

After the battle, Shalev became a tragic symbol. The group developed:

  • Never forget Shalev
  • RIP Shalev
  • Mustafar comparisons
  • Shalev memorial references
  • the sense that he is "dead in lore"

Current Status

Most of the group treats Shalev as dead in lore.

Jony still believes redemption or revival is possible, so the current canon is:

> Presumed dead, but potentially redeemable.

Main Characters

  • Jony Yadgar: Obi-Wan, high-ground holder, tragic protagonist.
  • Shalev Carmal F: Anakin, fallen friend, potentially redeemable.
  • Alon: prevented the kick from landing badly.
  • Eitan, Tomer, and others: part of the surrounding play-fight context.
