גדוד מטכלי

Tomer. Sticker artillery, chaos rhythm section, recurring main joke, and future imperial furniture.

Vibe

גדוד מטכלי is Tomer. Jony has known him for around six years, and his place in the group is basically as a recurring main joke: everyone roasts him constantly, but everyone still likes him.

He is one of the highest-volume sender labels across both chats. The account is everywhere: early gaming coordination, Shalev chants, security alerts, sticker-heavy runs, and later military/service jokes.

The גדוד מטכלי name itself is ironic Tomer lore. Tomer used to brag/flex to Yoav about going to some elite-sounding military role, repeating גדוד מטכלי like a status badge. Eventually it turned out he was basically doing storage/logistics work, permanently converting the phrase into fake-elite-energy canon.

Superpowers

  • Can turn repetition into comedy.
  • Heavy sticker deployment.
  • Excellent at short reactions that feel final.
  • Appears in both the silly old group and the later security group, which makes this label a bridge between eras.
  • Has a confirmed imperial destiny as sofa/carpet if Jony the Mad Scientist becomes Galactic Emperor.
  • Can oversell achievements until they become group terminology.

How They Speak

  • Short, reactive, high-frequency.
  • Big on repeated phrases and immediate agreement/disagreement.
  • Often behaves like the chat's percussion track.

Recurring Bits

  • שליוווווו escalation in Shalev mythology.
  • Tomer the sofa/carpet.
  • Sticker-heavy participation in Media, memes, and stickers.
  • Alert talk in Home-front alerts and shelters.
  • The 2026-04-24 color deletion incident.

Known Crimes

  • Being the designated object in too many jokes.
  • Excessive sticker fire.
  • Somehow making "לא נעים" feel like a legal ruling.
  • Fake elite military aura.
  • Trying to look cool/popular/conventional too hard.

Best Lore

  • In the imperial future, Tomer becomes the sofa/carpet.
  • More precisely: Tomer is sofa.
  • גדוד מטכלי means storage/logistics now. Sorry.
  • Despite the endless jokes, he is clearly liked and kept close to the group center.
