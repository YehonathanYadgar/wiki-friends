Etamar Zukin

Zuki: serious, humble, talented, slightly pick-me, and trying to stay composed while still being one of the main characters.

Vibe

Etamar appears across both groups, especially in the older AGI Vegas Group. He is less chaotic than the loudest participants and often functions as a stabilizer: checking times, asking if something is still relevant, and joining the actual activity after everyone finishes negotiating.

The direct chat with Jony adds a better layer: Etamar is a close practical friend, the kind of person you message for games, links, quick coordination, reactions, and small life logistics.

Group canon says Zuki is one of the central players. His archetype is "serious chad trying to act composed": talented and humble, but with a slight pick-me edge and some influence from a more serious friend group.

Superpowers

  • Brings the group back to the practical question.
  • Good at "still relevant?" energy.
  • Appears in the origin scenes without needing to dominate them.
  • Quietly important to the game-night machinery.
  • Maintains a high call-count friendship without needing to make it dramatic.
  • Serious-chad composure.
  • Quiet talent.
  • Tries to be above the chaos but remains absolutely in the chaos.

How He Speaks

  • Direct and practical.
  • More coordination than performance.
  • Drops reactions when the group needs a sane person in the room.
  • In DMs, often relaxed, quick, and gently teasing.

Known Crimes

  • Slight pick-me behavior.
  • Acting composed while being fully part of the nonsense.
  • Being influenced by a more serious friend group.
