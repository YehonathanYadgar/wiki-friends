# Rishon LeZion Dunes

> [!summary]
> The Mustafar of the friend group: sand, high ground, and the battlefield where Shalev fell into lore.

## Summary

The dunes around Rishon LeZion are the canonical site of the [2023 Great Battle Of Shalev](../events/2023-great-battle-of-shalev.md), during the final high school trip.

## Lore Significance

This is where [Jony](../people/jony-yadgar.md) had the high ground over [Shalev](../people/shalev-carmal-f.md), making the Anakin/Obi-Wan comparison unavoidable and historically correct.
