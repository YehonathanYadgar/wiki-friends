## Pattern

The direct chats are much more call-heavy and practical than the group chats. They include:

- "come play" invitations
- fast coordination
- voice/video calls
- links and media
- tiny school/army/life fragments
- private reactions to group chaos

## Why This Matters

Person pages should use direct chats to describe actual relationship texture, not just group performance.
