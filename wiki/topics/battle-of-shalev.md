# The Battle Of Shalev

> [!summary]
> The tragic civil-war arc: Jony and Shalev were close, then came the falling out, and the group turned it into Star Wars Mustafar mythology.

## Canon

[Shalev](../people/shalev-carmal-f.md) used to be a close friend of [Jony](../people/jony-yadgar.md). After a major falling out in 2023, known here as the [Great Battle of Shalev](../events/2023-great-battle-of-shalev.md), they stopped talking.

The battle happened during the final high school trip, in the [Rishon LeZion dunes](../places/rishon-lezion-dunes.md). Shalev wanted to fight Jony, Jony initially refused, and the "fun fight" escalated through dune-pushing, anger, a blocked punch, an accidental elbow, choking attempts, rolling down the dune, and a final kick that [Alon](../people/alon.md) helped deflect.

The group mythology around this became:

- `Never forget Shalev`
- `RIP Shalev`
- Anakin vs Obi-Wan on Mustafar comparisons
- endless tragic friendship-collapse lore

Canon roles:

- Shalev = Anakin
- Jony = Obi-Wan
- Reason: Jony had the high ground.

## Why It Matters

This is not just an inside joke; it explains why `שליוווווו` has so much power in the old chat. The group treats Shalev like a fallen-era symbol: funny, dramatic, and weirdly historic.
