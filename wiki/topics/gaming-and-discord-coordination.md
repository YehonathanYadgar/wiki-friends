# Gaming And Discord Coordination

> [!summary]
> The group sport is not gaming. The group sport is getting everyone to agree when gaming starts.

## Pattern

In [AGI Vegas Group](../groups/agi-vegas.md), gaming coordination is a recurring ritual:

- someone asks who is coming
- someone says they are eating, showering, working, walking a dog, or arriving home
- someone pushes the time earlier
- someone says ten is fine
- someone tells everyone to get into Discord

## Canon Games

- Age of Empires was the defining classic group game and a huge part of the old identity.
- Apex Legends is the current-era main game.
- Brawl, Minecraft, Discord, and ping pong also appear in the broader activity ecosystem, but Age is the historical anchor.
