# Alon And Yoav Beef

> [!summary]
> Former close friends, now not talking. Important enough to explain why [Alon](../people/alon.md) exists in the older group but not in the Judgment Day group.

## Canon

[Alon](../people/alon.md) and [Yoav](../people/yoav-hashriri.md) used to be close friends, but later stopped talking. This is the main reason Alon is not in [Chat 001](../groups/chat-001.md), even though the friend groups otherwise overlap.

## Why It Matters

This is a structural lore fact: it explains group membership, missing presence, and probably a lot of unspoken context in later chats.
