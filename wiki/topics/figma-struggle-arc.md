# Figma Struggle Arc

> [!summary]
> Jony cares about design enough to suffer. Auto layout, spacing, glow, ugly Hebrew UI, and the eternal question: does this look bad?

## Canon

[Jony](../people/jony-yadgar.md) cares about design more than most beginner developers. He notices when something looks default, old, empty, or badly spaced.

## Common Figma Moments

- "Where is the properties panel?"
- "Why is auto layout doing this?"
- "How do I add glow?"
- "How do I make equal spacing?"
- "Does this look ugly?"
- "Should the font be bigger?"
- "Why is there too much empty space?"

## Why It Matters

This is part of the builder arc. Jony does not just want the app to work; he wants it to look like it belongs in the robot future and not in an outdated Hebrew government form.
