# Animal-Based / Raw Meat Philosophy

> [!summary]
> Jony's health-optimization doctrine: meat, animal-based eating, anti-bread/pasta sentiment, raw-meat discussion, and warnings against blackpill-style decline.

## Canon

[Jony](../people/jony-yadgar.md) strongly pushes an animal-based diet direction:

- mainly meat
- eggs
- raw milk
- peppers
- cucumbers
- tomatoes
- avocados
- anti-bread
- anti-pasta
- raw-meat discussion
- anti-health-decline / anti-blackpill framing

This is one of the core sub-branches of [Jony the Mad Scientist](jony-the-mad-scientist.md): the body-optimization department.

## Why It Is Lore

The funny part is not only the diet itself. It is the way Jony treats health, civilization, decline, food, and optimization as one giant science project that the group is somehow expected to hear about.
