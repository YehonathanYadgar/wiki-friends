# Turkey Plastic Surgery Arc

> [!summary]
> One of Jony's classic mad-scientist schemes: analyze what surgeries everyone should get, then imagine the group going to Turkey together for optimization.

## Canon

[Jony](../people/jony-yadgar.md) once jokingly analyzed what plastic surgeries each friend should get and suggested that everyone go to Turkey together.

This belongs to the broader [Jony the Mad Scientist](jony-the-mad-scientist.md) pattern: half science, half optimization, half unlicensed group project. Yes, that is three halves.

## Why It Is Funny

It combines:

- group roast energy
- fake medical authority
- extreme optimization brain
- Turkey medical-tourism mythology
- Jony treating friendship like a redesign problem
