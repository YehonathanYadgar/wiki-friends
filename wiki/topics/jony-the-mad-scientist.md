# Jony The Mad Scientist

> [!summary]
> Jony as the AGI-obsessed chaos scientist: world-changing ideas, futuristic schemes, and the spiritual reason the group can be "on the road to AGI" while somehow stopping in Vegas first.

## Canon

[Jony](../people/jony-yadgar.md) is considered the group's crazy scientist / AGI guy. He constantly talks about AI, AGI, science, and dramatic future-changing ideas.

This directly explains the group name [AGI Vegas Group](../groups/agi-vegas.md): `בדרך ל AGI עוצרים בווגאס🍾🍾🍾` means that on the road to AGI, humanity apparently stops in Vegas first.

From the group's perspective, Jony behaves like AGI is three weeks away from robot gods. Nobody knows whether the timeline is real, but the intensity is real.

## Major Arcs

### AGI Robot Future

The central Jony trait: constant discussion about AGI, robots, future civilization, AI takeover/transformation, and post-human society. See [AGI / Robot Future Doctrine](agi-robot-future-doctrine.md).

### Emperor Arc

Jony's long-term plan is to become the first galactic emperor, build useful things, get rich, understand AI before the NPCs, and rule the galaxy with clean blue UI. See [Jony Emperor Arc](jony-emperor-arc.md).

### App Builder Arc

Jony builds AI/app projects, including [Emuna AI](emuna-ai-app-builder-arc.md), while fighting Apple, Figma, Supabase, Expo, TikTok, and App Store Connect.

### Turkey Plastic Surgery Arc

Jony once jokingly analyzed what surgeries each friend should get and suggested the group go to Turkey together. See [Turkey Plastic Surgery Arc](turkey-plastic-surgery-arc.md).

### Animal-Based / Raw Meat Philosophy

Jony pushes meat-heavy / animal-based health optimization, anti-bread and anti-pasta sentiment, raw-meat discussion, and anti-health-decline framing. See [Animal-Based / Raw Meat Philosophy](animal-based-raw-meat-philosophy.md).

## Superpowers

- Converts AI talk into friend-group mythology.
- Treats AGI like both a technical horizon and a comedy setting.
- Can make the future sound like a lab, empire, and bachelor party at once.

## Known Crimes

- Too many insane ideas.
- Galactic Emperor tendencies.
- Assigning [Tomer](tomer-the-sofa-carpet.md) to furniture duty.
- Acting like humanity is moments away from robot gods.
- Medical tourism group optimization.
- Meat-based civilization repair.
