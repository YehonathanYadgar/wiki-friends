# Tomer The Sofa / Carpet

> [!summary]
> When [Jony](../people/jony-yadgar.md) becomes Galactic Emperor, [Tomer](../people/gdud-matkali.md) does not become a minister, general, or duke. He becomes the imperial sofa/carpet. This is apparently settled law.

## Canon

The joke started during discussions about what everyone's role would be once [Jony](../people/jony-yadgar.md) becomes Galactic Emperor.

[Tomer](../people/gdud-matkali.md)'s archetype is trying very hard to look cool, seem popular/conventional, and signal status. So the group decided that in the Galactic Empire, Tomer becomes the imperial sofa.

Carpet exists as a secondary variation, but the canonical answer is:

> Tomer is sofa.

## Why It Matters

This is one of the clearest examples of the group's affection-through-roasting style. Tomer is a recurring main joke, but the joke works because he is liked and central enough to be assigned a permanent place in the empire.

It also captures the group's view of Tomer: someone who wants status, gets roasted for wanting status, and therefore receives the most humiliating official status possible.
