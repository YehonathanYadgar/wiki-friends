# Shalev Mythology

> [!summary]
> `שליו` is less a person in the transcript and more a summoning ritual performed by keyboard, powered by the tragic canon of [The Battle of Shalev](battle-of-shalev.md).

## Pattern

The export repeatedly contains elongated versions of `שליוווווו`, especially in the early days of [AGI Vegas Group](../groups/agi-vegas.md). It functions as:

- a call to join
- a group chant
- a hype signal
- a running bit
- a memorial flare for the post-falling-out era

## Canon Backstory

Shalev used to be close with [Jony](../people/jony-yadgar.md). After the 2023 falling out known as the [Great Battle of Shalev](../events/2023-great-battle-of-shalev.md), the group developed tragic Star Wars-style lore around him: "Never forget Shalev", "RIP Shalev", and Anakin vs Obi-Wan on Mustafar comparisons.

Canon status: presumed dead in lore, but potentially redeemable because Jony still believes revival is possible.
