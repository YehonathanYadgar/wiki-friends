# Eatan

> [!summary]
> The procedural philosopher of the group: schedules games, challenges reports, negotiates fairness, and occasionally writes like he is issuing a constitution for Discord.

## Vibe

Eatan is one of the biggest voices across both chats. He is the person who asks whether people are actually coming, pushes for timing that makes sense, and gives the group a layer of mock-serious structure.

He also has a strong "wait, is this reliable?" role during the security-update arcs, which makes him useful when the group starts digesting forwarded chaos.

Group canon answer on spelling/importance: "He doesn't matter." The wiki preserves `Eatan` because the exported sender label is funny and because not mattering is, spiritually, also lore.

## Superpowers

- Turns "who is playing?" into a formal negotiation.
- Notices when a forwarded WhatsApp report smells weird.
- Can sound like the attorney general of a Minecraft server.
- Keeps the group socially calibrated: fair, annoyed, funny, and somehow still organized.

## How He Speaks

- Longer than most people.
- Explanatory, slightly theatrical, and often mock-official.
- Uses fairness and procedure as comedy.
- Good at deadpan corrections.
