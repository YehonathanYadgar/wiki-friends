# Elad F

> [!summary]
> Reaction specialist. Shows up with heat, hearts, "גאט דאיימ", and the exact amount of commentary needed to certify that something has happened.

## Vibe

Elad appears in both chats and is especially visible as a responder: reacting to photos, events, and absurd group developments. He does not need the most messages to feel present; the style is compact and emphatic.

## Superpowers

- Certifies moments with short reactions.
- Brings English reaction slang into Hebrew chat flow.
- Good at making an image or event feel officially witnessed.

## How He Speaks

- Short.
- Expressive.
- Often reaction-first rather than essay-first.
