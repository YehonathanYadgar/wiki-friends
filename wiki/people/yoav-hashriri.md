# יואב השרירי💪

> [!summary]
> Muscle/gym guy, arrogant-but-funny chad, talented at basically everything, and one of the core main characters.

## Vibe

Yoav shows up strongly in both chats. In the older group, he is part of the initial `שליו` summoning and immediately gets pulled into questions of whether he is coming to play. In the later group, he becomes a recurring joke-character during security and media-heavy moments.

The direct chat changes the scale: Yoav is not just a bit in the group, he is one of the central one-on-one channels. The archive is dense with calls, stickers, images, Brawl/game summons, quick arguments, and rapid-fire "יואב" pings.

Group canon says Yoav is one of the real main characters: the muscle/gym archetype, talented at basically everything, arrogant in a funny way, and carrying elite chad energy.

## Superpowers

- Can make "yes, I am coming" feel like a plot twist.
- Naturally attracts group lore.
- Good at turning ordinary participation into a character arc.
- The emoji in the name is not decoration; it is branding.
- Somehow sustains both chaos and loyalty at 14,000-message scale.
- High-capacity call/sticker/media exchange node.
- Gym-bro authority.
- Talent-at-everything aura.
- Can be arrogant and still make it funny.

## How He Speaks

- Short bursts.
- Often direct and funny without over-explaining.
- Leans into the theatrical dynamic around him.
- In DMs, alternates between quick reassurance, blunt reactions, and being summoned like an emergency service.

## Known Crimes

- Being too talented.
- Arrogance with comedic shielding.
- Making gym-bro/chad energy central to the mythology.
- Former close-friend beef with [Alon](alon.md).
