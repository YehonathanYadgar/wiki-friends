# Shalev Carmal F

> [!summary]
> Less a normal participant, more a summonable entity. The group does not simply message Shalev; it chants him into existence.

## Vibe

Shalev has only 85 messages in [AGI Vegas Group](../groups/agi-vegas.md), but his name appears as a recurring group call. The `שליוווווו` motif is strong enough that he gets both a person page and a topic page: [Shalev mythology](../topics/shalev-mythology.md).

Canonically, Shalev used to be close with [Jony](jony-yadgar.md). Before the falling out, Shalev was generally quiet and not central to the whole group, but Jony liked him a lot because he was chill, unambitious, drama-free, and almost always available to play games.

After the [2023 Great Battle of Shalev](../events/2023-great-battle-of-shalev.md), they stopped talking and the group turned the collapse into tragic Star Wars lore.

## Superpowers

- Can become the center of a chat without sending the most messages.
- Generates elongated vowels at scale.
- Crashing/rejoining during games becomes part of the story, not a technical issue.
- Presumed dead in lore while still somehow potentially redeemable.

## How He Speaks

- Sparse in the export.
- Practical when present.
- Mostly defined by the group’s dramatic invocation of him.
