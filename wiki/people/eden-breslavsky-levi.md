# עדן ברסלבסקי לוי

> [!summary]
> The military-status auditor. If someone is getting too comfortable with gear, seniority, or self-image, Eden may appear to inspect the paperwork.

## Vibe

Eden becomes especially vivid in the military-service parts of the chats. He comments on service norms, seniority, bases, and whether someone has earned the right to act like that yet.

## Superpowers

- Can turn a gear discussion into a trial.
- Good at status checks and service-culture teasing.
- Brings "prove yourself" energy to the group.

## How He Speaks

- Direct, teasing, and rank-conscious.
- Often sounds like he is enforcing an unwritten military-social code.
