# Alon

> [!summary]
> Founder/admin energy, jiu-jitsu competence, medical-ish army role, and one side of the major Yoav beef.

## Vibe

Alon is central to [AGI Vegas Group](../groups/agi-vegas.md). The export says the group was created by Alon, and his messages often feel like he is either coordinating, moderating, or making the chat more dramatic on purpose.

Group canon adds the bigger context: Alon used to be close friends with [Yoav](yoav-hashriri.md), but they later stopped talking. That is why Alon is not in [Chat 001](../groups/chat-001.md). He is also very good at Jiu-jitsu and currently serves in the military in a sort of medic / medical-related role.

## Superpowers

- Can create a group and immediately make it feel like an institution.
- Uses moderation language as comedy.
- Excellent at short verdicts: someone is rude, someone gets muted, something is not pleasant.
- Strong side-quest generator in school/chat incidents.
- Jiu-jitsu competence.
- Medical-related army aura.

## How He Speaks

- Direct, compact, and dry.
- Often sounds like he has admin permissions even when the situation does not require admin permissions.
- Good at turning a small issue into an official incident.

## Known Crimes

- Admin energy.
- Former-Yoav-beef gravity.
- Making a group feel moderated even when it is just friends talking.
