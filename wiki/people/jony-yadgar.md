# Jony Yadgar

> [!summary]
> Ambitious coder. AGI prophet. Meat enjoyer. Figma struggler. Future galactic emperor.

## Basic Profile

- Name: Jony / Jonathan Yadgar
- Title: the first galactic emperor
- Main vibe: coder, builder, future ruler, AI guy, friend-group philosopher
- Current arc: army life, building stuff, learning tech, and preparing for the age of AGI robots

Jony is the friend who gets obsessed with a topic, thinks about it nonstop, explains it to everyone, and somehow turns it into either a product idea, a life doctrine, or a group meme.

## Vibe

Jony is ambitious, intense, funny, and a little insane in a productive way.

He is practical when building things, but dramatic when explaining why they matter. In [Chat 001](../groups/chat-001.md), he becomes the emergency broadcaster and war-room narrator. In [AGI Vegas Group](../groups/agi-vegas.md), he is part of the older gaming/social layer. Across the broader lore, he is the AGI guy, emperor candidate, and friend-group mad scientist.

- Turns technical confusion into a build plan.
- Converts AI anxiety into empire strategy.
